About

Name: Brian Casipit
School: San Francisco State University
Major: Computer Science
When I first entered college I was undecided on my major, but I knew I wanted to choose between Music, Engineering, and Computer Science.
I really thought about doing music because I was so interested in it coming out of high school, but I didn't know what I would do with a Music degree. I still like music, but I like keeping it as an interest and a hobby. Computer Science was intimidating at the time, so I decided to try out Electrical Engineering. After a while, I felt really uninterested and decided to switch to Computer Science. Ever since switching, I've felt much better.
I haven't been active in my hobbies for a while now, but I used to play guitar, do music production for fun, and dance.
Here's a snippet from the past. Within my group of friends, my specialty was bonebreaking--a form of contortion. My friend decided to make an intro tune for bonebreaking one day and showed it to me. This is a rough draft of what I put together in a few minutes. I don't bonebreak anymore and I would like to take good care of my joints from now on.
